Gear Checklist for Training

Bring a rifle with at least three magazines, 300 rounds of ammo, eye and ear pro, a plate carrier or rack will work. Wear durable clothing, bring water, snacks, a notebook, and a first aid kit (IFAK preferred). Specific gear requirements for private training sessions will be discussed on a case-by-case basis.
